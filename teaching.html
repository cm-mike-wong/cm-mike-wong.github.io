Dartmouth College Summer Hybrid Undergraduate Research (SHUR)

  • Summer 2022: Faculty, A computational approach to Legendrian knots
    In this iteration, students are working on exploring the use of Legendrian invariants in filtered grid homology to study Lagrangian cobordisms.
  • Summer 2021: Faculty, A graphical calculator for Legendrian knots
    I co-founded with Ina Petkova a summer research project in low-dimensional topology with a computer science component; in the first iteration, students worked on building a graphical user interface to automate manipulation of front diagrams of Legendrian knots. The software continues to be in development.
